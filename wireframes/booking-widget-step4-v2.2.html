Backend save

  • BookingController::store(): legge i 3 nuovi campi
  • Validazione birth_date: data nel passato, max 120 anni fa
  • Customer model: persiste i 4 campi (birth_date + 3 consent)
  • Audit: marketing_consent_source = 'booking_widget' + timestamp NOW()
